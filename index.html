Welcome to the Cancer Vaccine Institute!

The Cancer Vaccine Institute is the only national charity in the UK devoted exclusively to funding research into the use of cancer immunotherapy as a treatment for cancer. Larger cancer charities have recently become interested in this field and so you may have read about developments in the newspapers recently.
