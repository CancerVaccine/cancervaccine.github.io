Changing Lives Through Research

Transforming the future of cancer treatment

We are pioneers in researching cancer vaccines and immunotherapy to improve patients' survival rates and their quality of life for a wide range of cancers. 
We are dedicated to providing a tailored approach to all our patients giving them every possibility of a successful outcome.

CVI is a charity with ambition. Their vision is for cancer vaccines to become the standard treatment for improving survival rates and their patients' quality of life for a wide range of cancers. They are the only charity in the UK devoted exclusively to funding research into cancer immunotherapy & vaccines.
The charity is a small, approachable and transparent organisation driven by the pioneering and visionary work of the research team at St George's led by Professor Angus Dalgleish.
At the heart of the charity is the unique relationship they have with their patients, patients' families, fundraisers and donors. This has not only been built through the incredible clinical achievements and tangible results of the research team but through their attentive and tailored approach to each patient.     Find out more »
