Office Volunteers

We're so grateful to our amazing volunteers who give their time and use their experience and skills to help us fund our ground breaking research.

If you have some skills you’d like to share, whether they be fundraising, marketing, social media, web design, research or anything else, we’d love to hear from you.   We are based in Clapham but would love to talk to you wherever you live as we have some volunteers who work from home for us.

If  you would like to go on our volunteers’ database then please contact us.
